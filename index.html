Pork Giniling

A quick and easy dish for afternoon meals.

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork giniling
  • medium-sized potato
  • medium-sized carrot
  • 2pcs tomato (optional)
  • ground pepper
  • 1pc medium-sized onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • salt
  • 1/2tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 1/2tbsp sarsaya oyster sauce

Steps:

  1. Cut potato and carrot into cubes. Fry carrot until looks a little toasted, potato until transparent.
  2. Add pinch of salt and pepper, then set aside.
  3. Saute garlic for 30 seconds in medium heat. Add onion, then saute for another 30 seconds. Add sliced tomato (optional)
  4. Add pork-giniling.
  5. After pork-giniling looks cooked enough, add the soy sauce and oyster sauce. Mix thoroughly
  6. Add a pinch of salt and ground pepper.
  7. Add the carrots and potato.
  8. Ready to serve. Enjoy!
